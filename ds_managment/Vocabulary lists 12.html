About unit

  • Key Verb Phrases & Phrasal Verbs (V.2) также попадает в упражнения для подготовки к семинарам. Рекомендуем выучить и применять в обсуждениях.

Core Terminology & Concepts

Term Part of Speech Translation English Definition
Storytelling & Communication Strategy
Problem Statement Noun Phrase Постановка задачи A concise description of an issue that needs to be addressed or a process that needs improvement.
Data Story / Narrative Noun Phrase История данных / Нарратив A structured way of presenting findings that connects facts into a compelling, logical sequence to drive a decision.
Key Takeaway / Insight Noun Phrase Ключевой вывод / Инсайт The main, actionable conclusion drawn from data analysis, forming the core of the data story.
Call to Action (CTA) Noun Phrase Призыв к действию A clear, specific recommendation for what the audience should do next based on the data insights.
Executive Summary Noun Phrase Резюме для руководства A concise section at the beginning of a report summarizing key objectives, findings, and recommendations.
High-level Overview Noun Phrase Общий обзор A broad description of the project without small technical details.
Degrees of detail Noun Phrase Уровни детализации A framework for tailoring communication by adjusting the depth of information based on the audience's role and needs.
Subject-Matter Expert (SME) Noun Phrase Эксперт в предметной области A person with deep knowledge of the business domain (e.g., a senior marketing manager) who provides context and validates findings.
Lay audience (General audience) Noun Phrase Неподготовленная аудитория  An audience that lacks specialized knowledge or expertise in a particular technical, technological, or complex subject matter.
Target Audience / Decision-Maker Noun Phrase Целевая аудитория / Лицо, принимающее решение (ЛПР) The specific group or individual (e.g., product director, finance team) for whom the analysis is crafted, whose decisions or actions the report aims to influence.
End User (of a dashboard/report) Noun Phrase Конечный пользователь (дашборда/отчета) The person who will regularly interact with and consume the data product to monitor performance or inform their daily work.
Actionable Recommendation Noun Phrase Практическая рекомендация A specific, clear suggestion for what to do next, directly tied to the data insights.
Bottom line Noun Phrase Суть, итог, финансовый результат The most important point or the net financial impact; the ultimate "so what?" of the analysis.
North Star Metric Noun Phrase Ключевой показатель, метрика полярной звезды The single, crucial key performance indicator that's most predictive of a company's long-term success. To qualify as NSM a metric must: lead to revenue, reflect customer value, and measure progress.
Data Analysis & Statistical Concepts
Statistical Significance Noun Phrase Статистическая значимость A measure indicating that an observed effect or difference is unlikely to be due to random chance.
Confidence Interval Noun Phrase Доверительный интервал A range of values, estimated from data, that is likely to contain the true population parameter with a certain level of confidence.
Variance / Standard Deviation Noun Дисперсия / Стандартное отклонение Measures of how spread out the data points are around the mean.
Mean / Median / Mode Noun Среднее / Медиана / Мода Key measures of central tendency describing the "centre" of a dataset.
Skewness Noun Асимметрия, скошенность A measure of the asymmetry of the probability distribution of a variable around its mean.
Time Series Noun Phrase Временной ряд A sequence of data points indexed in time order (e.g., daily sales, hourly logs).
Segmentation / Cohort Noun Сегментация / Когорта An analytical strategy of pre-defining key subgroups in the data (for targeted analysis) to create comparative visuals that reveal differences in behaviour or performance.
Hypothesis Noun Гипотеза A testable statement about the relationship between variables that the analysis aims to validate or refute.
Root Cause Noun Phrase Первопричина The fundamental reason why a certain trend or problem is occurring.
Outlier / Anomaly Noun Выброс / Аномалия A data point that visually stands out from the overall pattern on a chart, often requiring special annotation or explanation in the narrative.
Causation Noun Причинно-следственная связь A relationship where one event directly brings about another. Much harder to prove than correlation.
Distribution Noun Распределение The way values of a variable are spread or dispersed (e.g., normal, skewed, uniform).
Granularity Noun Гранулярность, детализация The level of detail or aggregation in the data (e.g., daily vs. monthly, user-level vs. country-level).
Visualization Components & Tools
Data Point Noun Phrase Наблюдение / Экземпляр / Инстанс An individual unit of information, often a single mark on a chart.
Metric / KPI Noun Метрика / KPI A quantifiable measure used to track and assess performance.
Axis (X-axis / Y-axis) Noun Ось (ось X / ось Y) The reference lines defining the coordinate system on a chart.
Legend Noun Легенда A key explaining symbols, colours, or patterns in a chart.
Label Noun Подпись Text identifying an axis or the exact value of a data point.
Title / Caption Noun Заголовок / Подпись The main heading or descriptive text for a chart.
Filter / Slice Noun / Verb Фильтр / Срез A tool to interactively narrow down displayed data based on criteria.
Data Grid / Table Noun Phrase Таблица данных A structured format for displaying data in rows and columns.
Colour Palette Noun Phrase Цветовая палитра A coordinated set of colours for clarity and accessibility.
Data Density / Chart-junk Noun Phrase Информационная плотность / Визуальный мусор Useful info per unit area // Unnecessary, distracting visual elements.
Common Chart Types
Bar Chart (Vertical / Horizontal) Noun Phrase Столбчатая диаграмма (вертикальная / горизонтальная) Uses rectangular bars to represent and compare the magnitudes of different categories.
Line Chart / Line Graph Noun Phrase Линейный график Displays data points connected by straight line segments, ideal for showing trends over a continuous interval (usually time).
Scatter Plot Noun Phrase Точечная диаграмма Uses dots to represent values for two different numeric variables, excellent for revealing correlations or clusters.
Histogram Noun Гистограмма A bar chart that shows the distribution of a single numeric variable by grouping values into bins.
Box Plot (Box-and-Whisker Plot) Noun Phrase Ящик с усами Summarizes the distribution of a dataset, showing its median, quartiles, and potential outliers.
Pie Chart / Donut Chart Noun Phrase Круговая диаграмма / Кольцевая диаграмма Shows parts of a whole; best used for simple proportions (2-5 categories). Often criticized for poor precision.
Heatmap Noun Тепловая карта A matrix representation where individual values are denoted by colors, great for comparing two categorical dimensions and spotting patterns.
Area Chart Noun Phrase Диаграмма с областями Similar to a line chart, but the area below the line is filled with color, often used to show cumulative totals over time.
Stacked Bar Chart Noun Phrase Сложенная столбчатая диаграмма A bar chart where each bar is divided into sub-parts to show the contribution of different categories to the total.
Waterfall Chart Noun Phrase Каскадная диаграмма (водопад) Illustrates how an initial value is affected by a series of intermediate positive and negative values, leading to a final value.

Key Verb Phrases & Phrasal Verbs

Term Part of Speech Translation English Definition
To tell a data story / To build a narrative Verb Phrase Рассказывать историю данных / Выстраивать нарратив To structure and present findings in a logical, compelling sequence that leads to a clear conclusion or call to action.
To visualize / plot the data Verb Phrase Визуализировать данные / строить график To create a graphical representation (chart, graph) from a dataset.
To illustrate a point / trend Verb Phrase Проиллюстрировать мысль / тренд To use a chart or specific data point as evidence to make an argument or observation clearer and more convincing.
To highlight / point out a key insight Verb Phrase Выделить / указать на ключевой инсайт To draw the audience's attention to the most important finding or conclusion in the data.
To drill down into the data Phrasal Verb Углубиться в данные To interactively explore data at a more detailed level of granularity, often by applying filters.
To slice and dice the data Idiom (Verb Phrase) Разрезать и нарезать данные To analyse data from different perspectives and dimensions by applying various filters and groupings.
To compare A to B / against a benchmark Verb Phrase Сравнить А с Б / с бенчмарком To examine similarities and differences between datasets, metrics, or performance relative to a standard.
To track a metric / KPI over time Verb Phrase Отслеживать метрику / KPI во времени To monitor how a specific performance indicator changes, typically using a line chart.
To identify a trend / pattern / anomaly Verb Phrase Выявить тренд / паттерн / аномалию To discover a recurring direction, structure, or unusual outlier within the dataset.
To reveal a correlation (between X and Y) Verb Phrase Выявить корреляцию (между X и Y) To show a statistical relationship between two variables, often using a scatter plot.
To show the distribution of [data] Verb Phrase Показать распределение [данных] To display how values are spread across a range, typically using a histogram or box plot.
To break down the total by [category] Phrasal Verb Разбить общую сумму по [категории] To disaggregate a total figure into its constituent parts, e.g., using a stacked bar chart or pie chart.
To clean up / declutter a chart Phrasal Verb Очистить график от мусора To remove unnecessary elements (chartjunk) to improve readability and data density.
To annotate a chart Verb Phrase Добавить аннотации к графику To add explanatory text (labels, arrows, callouts) directly on the visualization to guide the audience.
To tailor the report to the audience Verb Phrase Адаптировать отчет под аудиторию To customize the level of detail, terminology, and visual style based on who will be viewing the report (e.g., executives vs. engineers).
To back up a claim with data Phrasal Verb Подкрепить утверждение данными To provide quantitative evidence or a specific visualization to support a statement or recommendation.
To walk someone through a dashboard Phrasal Verb Провести кого-либо по дашборду To explain the layout, key metrics, and interactive elements of a dashboard step by step.
To drive the point home Idiom (Phrasal Verb) Добить мысль, сделать вывод неоспоримым To make a conclusion or key takeaway completely clear and convincing, often by using a powerful visual or summarizing statement.
To leave them with a clear call to action Verb Phrase Оставить их с четким призывом к действию To conclude a presentation or report with a specific, actionable next step for the audience.
To follow a visual hierarchy Verb Phrase Следовать визуальной иерархии To arrange elements (size, color, position) so that the most important information is seen first.
To map data onto a chart Verb Phrase Сопоставить данные с графиком To assign specific data variables to visual channels like the x-axis, y-axis, or color.
To encode information visually Verb Phrase Визуально закодировать информацию To represent data values using visual properties like position, length, color, or shape.
To choose an appropriate chart type Verb Phrase Выбрать подходящий тип графика To select the most effective visual form (e.g., bar, line, scatter) for the specific data and message.
To overlay multiple series Verb Phrase Наложить несколько рядов данных To plot two or more data series on the same chart for direct comparison.
To smooth out noise in the data Verb Phrase Сгладить шум в данных To apply a technique (like moving average) to a line chart to reveal the underlying trend more clearly.
To zoom in on a specific time period Phrasal Verb Приблизить (увеличить) конкретный период To adjust the axis scale to focus the view on a detailed segment of the data, often after an overview.
To filter out irrelevant data Phrasal Verb Отфильтровать нерелевантные данные To exclude specific data points or categories that distract from the core message of the visualization.
To eyeball the data Verb (Idiom) Оценить данные на глаз To make a quick, informal assessment of trends or patterns just by looking at the chart.
To pick up on a trend Phrasal Verb Заметить, уловить тренд To notice and understand a developing pattern or direction in the data.
To tell a story with numbers Verb Phrase Рассказать историю с помощью чисел To go beyond just showing charts; to connect data points into a coherent, persuasive narrative.
To frame the findings Verb Phrase Подать, преподнести выводы To consciously present the insights within a specific context or perspective that influences interpretation.
To misinterpret a chart Verb Phrase Неверно интерпретировать график To draw an incorrect conclusion from a visualization, often due to a misleading design or lack of context.
To cherry-pick data Verb (Idiom) Выборочно использовать данные (подтасовывать) To selectively present only the data that supports a desired conclusion, while ignoring contradictory evidence.
To land the message Verb Phrase Донести сообщение To successfully ensure the main takeaway is understood and remembered by the audience.
To tie back to the business goal Phrasal Verb Cвязать все с бизнес-целью To explicitly connect the data insights and visualizations to the original objective or KPI.

Functional Language: Essential Phrases & Questions

Phrase / Construction Use Case Definition and Translation
Contrary to what we expected... — Contrary to what we expected, the new feature did not increase user engagement in our target segment. To frame a surprising finding that challenges team assumptions, immediately capturing attention and signaling an important insight.

— Вопреки нашим ожиданиям, новая функция не увеличила вовлеченность пользователей в целевом сегменте.

The data suggests that... — The data suggests that customers from Region B have a significantly higher lifetime value. To present a conclusion neutrally and professionally, attributing it to the data rather than personal opinion, which is less confrontational.

— Данные свидетельствуют о том, что клиенты из региона B имеют значительно более высокую пожизненную ценность.

This brings us to our next point. — We've seen the overall trend. This brings us to our next point: the drivers behind it. A classic presentation transition phrase that provides structure and signals a logical shift in the data narrative.

— Мы увидели общий тренд. Это подводит нас к следующему пункту: факторы, которые к нему привели.

The main takeaway here is... — The main takeaway here is the strong correlation between loading speed and user retention. To explicitly state the core insight from a chart or analysis, ensuring the audience does not miss the most important point.

— Главный вывод здесь — сильная корреляция между скоростью загрузки и удержанием пользователей.

Here's a high-level summary. For the full breakdown,... — Here's a high-level summary of our key findings. For the full breakdown with detailed calculations and segment analysis, see the appendix at the end of this report. To provide a concise overview for busy stakeholders while clearly directing them to supporting details, balancing accessibility with thoroughness.

— Вот краткий обзор наших ключевых выводов. Для полной детализации с подробными расчетами и анализом по сегментам смотрите приложение в конце этого отчета.

What this means for the business is... — Churn has dropped by 5%. What this means for the business is approximately $2M in annual retained revenue. The crucial translation layer that connects technical metrics to business value, revenue, or strategy, making the analysis relevant to stakeholders.

— Отток снизился на 5%. Что это значит для бизнеса — примерно $2 млн сохраненной годовой выручки.

Let’s look at the next steps. — That concludes the analysis. Let’s look at the next steps. First, we need to validate this hypothesis with an A/B test. To conclude a presentation with forward momentum, shifting the discussion from analysis to actionable tasks and ownership.

— На этом анализ завершен. Давайте взглянем на следующие шаги. Сначала нам нужно проверить эту гипотезу A/B-тестом.

Let me break down this graph for you.

Front and center is..

— Let me break down this graph for you. Front and center is our North Star metric: Daily Active Users (DAU). To initiate a guided explanation of a data visualization and immediately highlighting the primary performance indicator.

— Позвольте провести вас по этому дашборду. Вот ключевая метрика, которую мы отслеживаем — ежедневные активные пользователи (DAU).

As you can see from this chart, [metric] has shown... — As you can see from this chart, conversion rate has shown a steady upward trend over the quarter. To verbally narrate a clear visual pattern, helping the audience interpret the chart and reinforcing the key message of growth or decline.

— Как видно из этого графика, конверсия демонстрирует устойчивый рост в течение квартала.

I'd like to draw your attention to this area of the chart... — I'd like to draw your attention to this area of the chart. It highlights a potential anomaly in late October. To act as a visual guide, focusing the audience's eye on a specific, possibly problematic detail that requires investigation.

— Я хочу обратить ваше внимание на эту область графика. Здесь видна потенциальная аномалия в конце октября.

To put this in perspective, this value is... — Our error rate is 2.3%. To put this in perspective, this value is 30% above our benchmark. To provide an interpretive frame for a number, making it meaningful by comparing it to a known standard, which is essential for proper performance evaluation.

— Наш уровень ошибок — 2,3%. Для контекста: это значение на 30% выше нашего бенчмарка.

If we drill down into this segment, we can see that... — If we drill down into this segment, we can see that the driver is primarily a specific pricing plan. To demonstrate analytical depth and show the ability to move from high-level trends to root-cause analysis by exploring data at a finer granularity.

— Если мы углубимся в этот сегмент, то увидим, что основным драйвером является конкретный тарифный план.

This scatter plot reveals a strong correlation between... — This scatter plot reveals a strong correlation between session length and customer satisfaction score. To interpret a statistical relationship observed in a visualization, framing it as an opportunity for further analysis while carefully avoiding claims of causation.

— Эта точечная диаграмма показывает сильную корреляцию между длительностью сессии и оценкой удовлетворенности клиента.

While the overall trend is positive, this breakdown shows... — While the overall trend is positive, this breakdown shows that growth is uneven across product lines. To present a balanced, sophisticated analysis that acknowledges a high-level success while revealing underlying complexities or risks.

— Хотя общий тренд положительный, эта разбивка показывает, что рост неравномерен по продуктовым линейкам.

I want to be careful not to misinterpret this... — I want to be careful not to misinterpret this. The chart shows correlation, not necessarily causation. To demonstrate statistical rigor and intellectual honesty, preempting a common logical fallacy and building credibility with a technically savvy audience.

— Хочу быть осторожным, чтобы не сделать ложных выводов. График показывает корреляцию, но не обязательно причинно-следственную связь.

Before we look at the aftermath, let's examine… this is where the clues are hidden. — Before we look at the aftermath, let's examine the scene right before the event. I'll set our time filter to capture the final two weeks of 'normal' operation—this is where the clues are hidden. To lead a live, interactive analysis session, demonstrating how to use dashboard tools to investigate anomalies and uncover temporal dynamics.

— Прежде чем смотреть на последствия, давайте изучим сцену непосредственно перед событием. Я установлю наш временной фильтр на последние две недели «нормальной» работы — именно здесь скрываются подсказки.

The story this data tells is that... — The story this data tells is that a marketing campaign (X) increased awareness, which led to a sign-up spike (Y), and now we see improved retention (Z). To explicitly craft a causal narrative from disparate data points, transforming a series of charts into a compelling cause-and-effect story.

— История, которую рассказывают эти данные, такова: рекламная кампания (X) повысила узнаваемость, что привело к всплеску регистраций (Y), и теперь мы видим улучшенное удержание (Z).

To make this chart clearer,... — To make this chart clearer, I'll clean it up by removing the gridlines and adding direct labels. To articulate the principles of good visualization design in real-time, showing intentionality in reducing chartjunk and improving clarity.

— Чтобы сделать этот график понятнее, я его упрощу: уберу сетку и добавлю прямые подписи.

So, what does this all mean for our strategy? — So, what does this all mean for our strategy? In practical terms, it means we need to focus on retaining our high-value segment. To perform the critical role of a strategic advisor, synthesizing analysis into a direct, business-oriented implication for decision-makers.

— Итак, что все это значит для нашей стратегии? Практически это означает, что нам нужно сосредоточиться на удержании нашего высокодоходного сегмента.

Before we dive into the details, here's the big picture... — Before we dive into the details, here's the high-level view: revenue is up, but profitability is under pressure. To establish a "glide path" for the audience, providing a strategic overview first to create a mental framework for absorbing detailed analysis later.

— Прежде чем углубиться в детали, вот общая картина: выручка растет, но рентабельность под давлением.

This isn't just a random spike. Let me overlay... — This isn't just a random spike. Let me overlay the marketing campaign timeline to show the cause. To move from observing a pattern to demonstrating causality by adding a contextual data layer, strengthening the argument with direct evidence.

— Это не просто случайный всплеск. Позвольте мне наложить график маркетинговой кампании, чтобы показать причину.

The real story isn't in the average; it's in this long tail... — The real story isn't in the average; it's in this long tail of the distribution where we find our most engaged users. To shift focus from summary statistics to impactful segments, revealing insights that are hidden by averages and highlighting opportunities or risks in the tails.

— Настоящая история — не в среднем значении, а в этой длинной хвостовой части распределения, где мы находим наших самых вовлеченных пользователей.

I've kept the design minimal to avoid chart-junk... — I've kept the design minimal to avoid chart-junk and let the data speak for itself. To professionally defend a minimalist aesthetic by invoking Tufte's principles, showing that design choices are driven by a desire for maximal data density.

— Я сделал дизайн минималистичным, чтобы избежать визуального мусора и позволить данным говорить самим за себя.

For clearer comparison, consider using… — For clearer comparison, consider using a bar chart instead. Its common baseline makes it significantly easier to visually compare segment sizes and estimate the differences between market shares. To provide constructive feedback on visualization choices during a review, offering a principled alternative that improves visual accuracy.

— Для более наглядного сравнения рекомендую использовать столбчатую диаграмму. Единая база (линия оси) позволяет гораздо проще сопоставлять размеры сегментов и оценивать разницу между долями рынка.

So, to recap and drive the point home... — So, to recap and drive the point home: our north star metric is up, but this breakdown reveals a vulnerability in our European market. To deliver a powerful, memorable closing summary that balances good news with a critical caveat, ensuring the key strategic takeaway is unambiguous.

— Итак, подводя итог и чтобы окончательно донести мысль: наш ключевой показатель растет, но эта детализация выявляет уязвимость на европейском рынке.
